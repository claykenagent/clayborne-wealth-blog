This post includes the full template and instructions. Download it, customize it, and start tracking today.

## Why You Need to Track Your Net Worth

Your net worth is the single most important financial metric. It's the difference between everything you own (assets) and everything you owe (liabilities).

Net Worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities

Tracking net worth monthly does three things:

1. **Shows real progress** - When you see your net worth increase by $2,000 in 30 days, motivation skyrockets
2. **Reveals spending patterns** - Most people have no idea where their money goes. Tracking exposes leaks
3. **Keeps you accountable** - Knowing you'll review your finances monthly changes behavior

Studies show people who track their finances accumulate 2-3x more wealth than those who don't. It's the cheapest, most effective financial strategy available.
