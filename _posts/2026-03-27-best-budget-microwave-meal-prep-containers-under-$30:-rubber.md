## What to Look For: The Specs That Actually Matter

Before we dive into specific products, let's talk about what separates leak-proof winners from disappointing duds:

**1. Seal Quality**
The best budget containers use silicone-lined lids with locking tabs. These create an airtight seal without the $40 price tag of premium brands. Look for containers with at least 2-point or 3-point locking mechanisms.

**2. Microwave & Dishwasher Safe**
Your container should survive 200+ wash cycles *and* repeated microwave use. BPA-free plastic rated for microwave heating is non-negotiable.

**3. Durability Testing**
Real-world durability = surviving corner drops, temperature changes, and repeated flexing. Customer reviews mentioning "still using after a year" or "survived 100+ washes" matter more than marketing claims.

**4. Size Flexibility**
The sweet spot for busy professionals? Containers in the 32–40 oz range (approximately 4–5 cups). Large enough for a real meal, small enough to fit in lunch bags and work fridges.
