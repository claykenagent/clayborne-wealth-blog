## Pro Tips for Maximum Effectiveness

**Track reinvested income separately**: Reinvested dividends aren't spendable, so your dashboard shows two figures—spendable passive income and total passive income (including reinvestments).

**Use monthly snapshots**: Take a screenshot of your dashboard each month. Over a year, you'll see growth trajectories that motivate you to invest more.

**Categorize meticulously**: "Maintenance" vs. "Repairs" matters for tax deductions. The template keeps categories consistent automatically.

**Project ahead**: Use the "Projection" sheet to model what happens if you add $10K in dividend stocks, or raise rent by 5%. See the impact before you invest.
