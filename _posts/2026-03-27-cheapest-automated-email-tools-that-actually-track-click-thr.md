## Four Budget Email Tools with Real Click Tracking (Compared)

I've tested each of these tools personally. Here's what you need to know:

### 1. **Mailchimp** — Best for Pure Affordability ($5–20/month)

**What it costs:** Free up to 500 contacts, then $5–20/month for small lists.

**What click tracking includes:**
- Click maps showing which links were clicked (and how many times)
- Detailed reports per campaign
- Segmentation based on click behavior
- A/B testing with click-rate tracking

**Why it works for solopreneurs:**
Mailchimp has been the default for a reason. It's intuitive, it doesn't feel cheap, and the click tracking is *actually useful*. You can see at a glance that 24 people clicked link A and 8 clicked link B. Then you adjust your strategy accordingly.

**The catch:** Design templates feel a bit dated. Mobile responsiveness is "okay," not "gorgeous."

**Best for:** Service providers, consultants, coaches who prioritize data over design.

[Get started with Mailchimp here](https://mailchimp.com)

---

### 2. **Beehiiv** — Best for Content Creators ($13/month)

**What it costs:** $13/month (no free tier, but comes with free landing pages and referral bonuses).

**What click tracking includes:**
- Detailed click analytics with CTR % by link
- Reader engagement score
- Segment tracking (which readers clicked what)
- Custom link tracking and UTM builder

**Why it works for solopreneurs:**
If you're building an audience (newsletter, blog, digital products), Beehiiv is designed for *growth* first, monetization second. The click tracking is granular—you see not just *that* people clicked, but *which type of person* clicked what. That's segmentation gold.

**The catch:** Pricing jumps steeply at higher tiers. You're paying more than Mailchimp, but you get a more modern platform.

**Best for:** Newsletter writers, course creators, anyone building a personal brand.

[Start your Beehiiv free trial here](https://beehiiv.com)

---

### 3. **ConvertKit** — Best for Course Creators ($15+/month)

**What it costs:** $15/month (free tier exists but is very limited).

**What click tracking includes:**
- Click-through rates on all links in emails
- Subscriber tagging based on clicks
- Sequences that trigger based on link clicks
- Revenue tracking (if you're selling digital products)

**Why it works for solopreneurs:**
ConvertKit was built by bloggers, for bloggers. It assumes you're trying to sell something (a course, a service, a digital product). The click tracking isn't just data—it's *actionable*. When someone clicks a link to your sales page, they get tagged. That tag can trigger a follow-up sequence. That follow-up converts them.

**The catch:** Limited automation on the cheapest plan. You're not building complex workflows for $15/month.

**Best for:** Digital product creators, online course instructors, affiliate marketers.

[Join ConvertKit here](https://convertkit.com)

---

### 4. **ActiveCampaign** — Best for Serious Automation ($9/month entry)

**What it costs:** $9/month for the "Lite" plan (entry-level automation).

**What click tracking includes:**
- Click tracking on every email link
- Advanced segmentation based on clicks
- Full CRM integration (see click data linked to contact profiles)
- Automation workflows triggered by clicks

**Why it works for solopreneurs:**
ActiveCampaign is the "professional" tool at a budget price. You get enterprise features (automation, CRM, lead scoring) without the enterprise price tag. Click tracking is paired with automation—meaning you can create powerful workflows based on engagement.

**The catch:** Steeper learning curve. Interface feels more complex. Best if you're willing to spend a few hours learning.

**Best for:** E-commerce sellers, service providers with sales pipelines, anyone who needs CRM + email combined.

[Get started with ActiveCampaign here](https://www.activecampaign.com)
