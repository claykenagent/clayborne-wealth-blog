## Your Complete Micro-Budget Wealth Tracker Template

### **SHEET 1: Daily Log**

Copy this structure into Google Sheets or Excel:

| Date | Category | Type | Amount | Notes | Running Balance |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|-----------------|
| 1/15/2025 | Side Hustle | Income | +$12.50 | Freelance writing | $12.50 |
| 1/15/2025 | Expense | Spending | -$3.00 | Coffee | $9.50 |
| 1/16/2025 | Reselling | Income | +$8.00 | Sold textbook | $17.50 |
| 1/16/2025 | Investment | Micro-Investment | +$5.00 | Moved to savings | $12.50 |
| 1/17/2025 | Gig Work | Income | +$20.00 | Task completion | $32.50 |

**Categories to track:**
- Side Hustle (freelance, consulting, part-time)
- Gig Work (DoorDash, TaskRabbit, etc.)
- Reselling (eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Poshmark)
- Interest/Gains (savings interest, dividends, referral bonuses)
- Expense (daily spending to subtract)
- Micro-Investment (money moved to savings/brokerage)

**Formula for Running Balance (Column F):**
```
=IF(ROW()=2, D2, F1+D2)
```
This creates a cumulative total that updates automatically.

### **SHEET 2: Analytics Dashboard**

**Wealth Velocity Chart** (update weekly)

This shows your average daily income growth:

| Week | Total Income | Total Expenses | Net Growth | Daily Average |
|------|--------------|----------------|------------|----------------|
| Week 1 (Jan 8-14) | $45.00 | -$18.00 | $27.00 | $3.86 |
| Week 2 (Jan 15-21) | $52.50 | -$22.00 | $30.50 | $4.36 |
| Week 3 (Jan 22-28) | $68.00 | -$19.50 | $48.50 | $6.93 |

**Expense Ratio** (metric that matters):

```
Expense Ratio = Total Expenses ÷ Total Income

Week 1: $18 ÷ $45 = 40% (you're spending 40% of earnings)
Week 2: $22 ÷ $52.50 = 42% (slightly worse)
Week 3: $19.50 ÷ $68 = 29% (improving!)
```

The goal: keep this under 25% if you're building wealth.

**Compound Projection** (why daily logging matters):

Assuming your weekly growth rate continues:

```
Current Balance: $50
Weekly Average Growth: $5.50

4 weeks: $72
12 weeks: $116
26 weeks (6 months): $193
52 weeks (1 year): $336
```

This is just from tracking micro-income. Real money.
