## The Zero-to-$100/Month Passive Income Tracking Template

Here's the system broken down into three core sections:

### **Section 1: Revenue Stream Registry**

This is your master list of every money-making channel. Create a simple table with:

| Stream Name | Platform | Launch Date | Current Status | Monthly Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amazon Affiliate – Home Gadgets | Blog Posts | Jan 15, 2024 | Active | $25 |
| Gumroad Digital Products | Email List | Feb 1, 2024 | Active | $40 |
| AdSense (Blog) | Website | Dec 1, 2023 | Active | $15 |
| Skillshare Referrals | Social Media | Mar 1, 2024 | Testing | $10 |
| Medium Partner Program | Articles | Jan 20, 2024 | Active | $10 |

**Why this matters:** You need a baseline inventory. Many creators think they have 2-3 income streams but actually have 8. This sheet forces clarity on what you're actually running.

### **Section 2: Daily/Weekly Outcome Log**

This is where the magic happens. Every time you take a promotional action or check platform payouts, you log it here:

| Date | Stream | Action Type | Detail | Outcome | Payout Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 2024-03-15 | Amazon Affiliate | Posted Review | Best Standing Desks Article | 3 clicks, 1 purchase ($8 commission) | Pending |
| 2024-03-15 | Gumroad | Email Campaign | Weekly Newsletter Feature | 12 clicks, 2 sales ($9.98 revenue) | Paid |
| 2024-03-16 | Medium | New Article | "How to Start Passive Income" | 45 reads (est. $2.25) | Pending |
| 2024-03-18 | AdSense | Organic Traffic | Blog SEO | ~200 impressions (est. $0.40) | Pending |
| 2024-03-20 | Skillshare | Social Post | TikTok Link Share | 8 clicks, 0 sign-ups | Pending |

**What you're tracking:**
- **Action Type:** What you *did* (posted content, sent email, shared link)
- **Detail:** Specificity matters. "Posted review" is vague. "Posted standing desk review comparing to Flexispot" is useful.
- **Outcome:** The measurable result (clicks, conversions, revenue)
- **Payout Status:** Tracks whether you've been paid yet (critical for reconciliation)

### **Section 3: Monthly Reconciliation Dashboard**

At the end of each month, create a summary:

| Stream | Estimated Revenue | Actual Payouts | Conversion Rate | Payouts Received |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amazon Affiliate | $12.50 | $0 | 2.1% | Pending (March payout) |
| Gumroad | $29.85 | $29.85 | 4.2% | Yes |
| Medium | $8.75 | $0 | 3.1% | Pending (April payout) |
| AdSense | $5.40 | $0 | 0.8% | Pending (threshold not met) |
| Skillshare | $0 | $0 | 0% | N/A |
| **TOTAL** | **$56.50** | **$29.85** | – | – |

**This tells you:**
- Which streams are actually paying out (not just "pending")
- Which have the highest conversion rates (double down here)
- Where payment delays exist (some platforms pay 30-60 days later)
- Which experiments aren't working (Skillshare in this example)

## How to Use This Template (3 Simple Steps)

### **Step 1: Set It Up (15 minutes)**
Copy the three sections above into a Google Sheet or Excel file. Customize the "Stream Name" and "Platform" rows to match your actual income sources.

**Pro tip:** If you use [Google Sheets](https://sheets.google.com), you can add conditional formatting to highlight streams that haven't generated payouts in 30 days. This forces you to either optimize or kill underperformers.

### **Step 2: Log Weekly (10 minutes)**
Every Friday, spend 10 minutes reviewing your week:
- Check each platform for new conversions
- Log promotional actions you took
- Note outcomes and estimated earnings

Don't aim for perfection. If you can't remember the exact number of clicks, estimate. The goal is pattern visibility, not accounting-grade precision.

### **Step 3: Review Monthly (20 minutes)**
On the last Friday of each month, fill in the reconciliation dashboard. Ask yourself:
1. Which stream performed best?
2. Which stream should I kill?
3. Which stream deserves more attention?

This single question—**What gets more attention next month?**—is where most creators fail. They don't iterate based on data.
